Masala Fries

Prep Time

30 min

Total Time

45 mins

Yield

6 servings

Ingredients

Masala Fries
  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 4 cups water for soaking
  • 2 tablespoons oil>
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoongaram masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon chat masala optional
Siracha Mayo
  • 1 cup mayo
  • 3 tablespoons Sriracha chili sauce
  • Juice from 1/2 a lemon (about 2 tablespoons)
  • 1 garlic clove, finely minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Ketchup

INSTRUCTIONS

Masala Fries

1. Rinse and pat dry the potatoes before you slice them (photo 1).

2. Cut the potatoes into 4-inch long and ⅓-inch thick sticks that are all the same size (photos 2 and 3).

3. Soak the cut potato in a bowl of water for at least 15 minutes (photo 4).

4. After 15 minutes, drain the potatoes, wipe them dry, and place them in a large mixing bowl (photos 5 and 6).

5. Add the spices on top of the potato slices and stir to combine evenly over them (photos 7 and 8).

6. Preheat airfryer 400 F

7. Bake the fries in the air fryer at 380°F or 190°C for 25 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through cooking (photos 9 and 10).

8. Enjoy the fries hot!

Dipping Sauce:
  • 1. Combine siracha mayo ingredients
  • 2. Serve siracha mayo or ketchup
