Brussel Sprouts

Prep Time

10 mins

Total Time

30 mins

Yield

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Brussel Sprouts
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder (optional)
  • 3 turns of fresh black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

Brussel Sprouts:

1. Preheat oven to 400 Degrees F

2. Cut Brussel sprouts stem and in half

3. Place flat (inside on pan)

4. Cook in 400 degree F oven for 15 minutes

5. After 15 min shake pan around then place for additional 11-13 minutes or until outer layer is burnt/crisp

6. Serve in a bowl and sprinkle kosher salt for presentation and taste
