Bruscetta

Prep Time

5 mins

Total Time

10 mins

Yield

30 servings

Ingredients

  • 6-7 ripe plum or beefsteak tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 small red onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • olive oil spray
  • 6-8 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 16 oz baguette or French bread
  • 1-2 garlic cloves, peeled

INSTRUCTIONS

Bruscetta:

1. Combine tomatoes, chopped garlic, red onion, vinegar, oil, salt, pepper, and basil.

2. Set aside

3. Preheat oven on broil

4. Slice bread into 1/2 inch thick slices.

5. Place on a tray and spray lightly with olive oil.

6. Toast bread under broiler, until golden, watching closely not to let it burn.

7. When bread is toasted, rub each piece with whole garlic clove.

8. Place bread on a platter and top with tomato mixture.

9. Serve immediately or the bread will become soggy.
