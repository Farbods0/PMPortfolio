Styles of Food

Appetizer

A small portion of food served before the main part of a meal in order to stimulate the appetite. Recipes are intended to be made quickly so that you can entertain guests while preparing for the main course. They should, therefore, be made attractive in appearance and tempting in flavor

Entree

The main event! Recipes are from around the world to satisify any craving and occasion. Some recipes can be whipped up in a day some take a few, but all will taste great.

Dessert

The end of the party. Desserts can be smooth, tangy, crumbly, decadent, etc. Choose a dessert that fits your need and always save room!
